AWS Service

AWS works a little different to the other two services, you have to upload your picture first from the local environemnt to a so called cloud bucket

This bucket then contains your image you'd like to analyze.

1. For this service you have to provide your accesskey, secretkey and a existing bucket on your S3 storage

2. Then you choose a picture from your environemnt and continue with the upload button

3. After succssful upload to the bucket you can continue with clicking the analyte button

4. The analyze function takes your uploaded picture and runs the aws analyze service

5. You will get a .json response from the API

The response might not show up when clicking the analyze button once, just try a second time then it should work :)
It might be a issue with the callback function which we are not able to solve :/ tried a lot
